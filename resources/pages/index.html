What is WOW?

WOW is an integrated ERP system connecting workers, Service Providers and Producers throughout the Oil & Gas industry. WOW ERP system enhances the infrastructure for Oil & Gas Producers and Service Providers by reducing redundancy and increasing productivity in daily business operations (i.e. HR, Safety, Accounting, Engineering, Project Management and Quality Assurance).
